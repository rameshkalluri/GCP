# Day 9 — Operations, IaC & Data/AI Intro

Notes for Day 9 of the [10-Day GCP Learning Plan](../GCP-10-Day-Learning-Plan.md).

> **Big-picture analogy:** Once your city (infrastructure) is built, you need a
> **control room** watching every street (monitoring/logging), **blueprints** so you can
> rebuild the city exactly (Infrastructure as Code), and **smart analysts** who turn
> mountains of data into insight (BigQuery) and predictions (Vertex AI).

## Topics
1. [Monitoring & Logging](01-monitoring-and-logging.md) — Cloud Monitoring, Logging, Error Reporting, Trace.
2. [Observability: SLOs & Alerts](02-observability-slos-alerts.md) — uptime checks, alerts, dashboards, SLIs/SLOs.
3. [Infrastructure as Code](03-infrastructure-as-code.md) — Terraform, Deployment Manager, Config Connector.
4. [BigQuery & Data](04-bigquery-and-data.md) — serverless analytics, Pub/Sub, Dataflow.
5. [Vertex AI & ML Intro](05-vertex-ai-and-ml.md) — a quick tour of ML on GCP.

## Day 9 Goals
- [ ] Understand Cloud Monitoring, Logging, and Error Reporting.
- [ ] Know what SLIs/SLOs and alerts are.
- [ ] Explain Infrastructure as Code and why Terraform is used.
- [ ] Understand what BigQuery is and when to use it.
- [ ] View logs/metrics dashboards, provision a VM with Terraform, run a BigQuery query.
