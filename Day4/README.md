# Day 4 — Networking (VPC)

Notes for Day 4 of the [10-Day GCP Learning Plan](../GCP-10-Day-Learning-Plan.md).

> **Big-picture analogy:** Networking is the **roads, gates, and addresses** of your
> cloud city. The **VPC** is the whole **private road system**, **subnets** are
> **neighborhoods**, **firewall rules** are **security gates**, **IP addresses** are
> **house addresses**, and a **load balancer** is the **traffic cop** directing cars
> to the right building.

## Topics
1. [VPC & Subnets](01-vpc-and-subnets.md) — the private road network & neighborhoods.
2. [Firewall Rules & Routes](02-firewall-rules-and-routes.md) — gates and road signs.
3. [IP Addresses & Cloud NAT](03-ip-addresses-and-nat.md) — house addresses & going online privately.
4. [Load Balancing](04-load-balancing.md) — the traffic cop spreading the load.

## Day 4 Goals
- [ ] Explain what a VPC and subnet are in plain English.
- [ ] Understand firewall rules (allow/deny) and routes.
- [ ] Know internal vs external IPs and what Cloud NAT does.
- [ ] Understand why and when to use a load balancer.
- [ ] Create a custom VPC + subnet + firewall, and a load balancer over 2 VMs.
