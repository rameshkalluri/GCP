# Day 2 — IAM & Security Basics

Notes for Day 2 of the [10-Day GCP Learning Plan](../GCP-10-Day-Learning-Plan.md).

> **Big-picture analogy:** Think of GCP as a big **office building**.
> Security (IAM) is the system of **ID cards, keys, and door locks** that decides
> *who* can enter *which* rooms and *what* they're allowed to do inside.

## Topics
1. [IAM Deep Dive](01-iam-deep-dive.md) — members, roles, policies (the hotel analogy).
2. [Service Accounts & Keys](02-service-accounts-and-keys.md) — IDs for robots/apps.
3. [Least Privilege & Security Best Practices](03-least-privilege-and-best-practices.md).

## Day 2 Goals
- [ ] Explain who/what/where of IAM in plain English.
- [ ] Know the difference between primitive, predefined, and custom roles.
- [ ] Understand service accounts and why keys are risky.
- [ ] Apply "least privilege" thinking.
- [ ] Grant and revoke a role with `gcloud`.
